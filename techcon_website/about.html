Our History

TechCon began in 2018 as a small gathering of local tech enthusiasts, aiming to create a platform for knowledge sharing and networking. Over the years, it has grown exponentially, attracting thousands of attendees and prominent speakers from around the globe. Each year, we strive to push the boundaries of technological discourse and foster a vibrant community of innovation.

(Image: TechCon's humble beginnings in 2018)

Our Mission

Our mission at TechCon is to empower individuals and organizations with the knowledge and connections needed to thrive in the digital age. We aim to:

  • Educate: Provide cutting-edge insights and practical skills through diverse sessions and workshops.
  • Inspire: Showcase groundbreaking innovations and thought leadership to spark creativity.
  • Connect: Facilitate networking opportunities between professionals, startups, and investors.
  • Innovate: Encourage the development and adoption of new technologies for a better future.

Past Speakers

We are proud to have hosted some of the brightest minds in technology. Here are a few notable speakers from previous TechCon events:

Dr. Anya Sharma (TechCon 2022)

Dr. Sharma is a leading AI Ethicist known for her work on responsible AI development and policy. Her keynote at TechCon 2022 focused on the societal impact of artificial intelligence.

Mr. Ben Carter (TechCon 2021)

A renowned cybersecurity expert, Mr. Carter shared insights on advanced persistent threats and strategies for digital defense. His session was highly acclaimed for its practical advice.

Ms. Clara Lee (TechCon 2020)

Ms. Lee, a pioneer in cloud computing solutions, discussed the future of decentralized cloud infrastructure. Her vision continues to influence many in the industry.
